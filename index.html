What You're Paying Per Hour

The MSP model is billed per user, not per hour. This shows what that translates to on an hourly basis each month, compared to Techcierge's standard project rate of $160/hr.

Effective Hourly Rate vs Standard Rate ($160/hr)

How to read this

The effective hourly rate is calculated by dividing the monthly MSP charge by total recorded support hours, including the recurring 5 hours of monthly maintenance built into the model.

Across all 8 months of the pilot, the effective rate ranged from $41/hr to $84/hr. Techcierge's standard project rate is $160/hr. The MSP model delivered support at a significant discount to that rate every single month.
